The Sea

Rent a sailboat and explore the islands around or travel beyond the islands and visit the Baltic sea. Or you can rent a canoe and just relax while paddling between the islands.

City

Visit the old city of Vaxholm and learn more about the Swedish culture. Shop at local vendors and enjoy summer City of Vaxholm. In the evening you can dine at the local sports bar and enjoy watching a game together with your dinner.

Camping

Come and camp in Vaxholm. The camping site is right beside Vaxholms skypark and the beach. Walking to city centre takes a mere 20 minutes or just 3 minutes if you take the bus.
